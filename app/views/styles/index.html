Styles

Services for government users in the Home Office (new services and existing digital services that are being redeveloped) should use these styles.

In the Home Office this includes:

  • caseworking systems
  • microsites
  • intranets

Why we created these styles

If your service isn’t on a gov.uk or service.gov.uk domain, you can’t use the GOV.UK crown logo (Service Manual, Services for government users) or the GDS Transport font.

Most Home Office services for Government users fall within these restrictions. For those services, these styles replace the GOV.UK styling.

This guidance applies to digital services, and does not affect or change any of the Home Office’s existing brand advice for print and offline applications.
