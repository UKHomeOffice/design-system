What can I search? You can search for all data across reports. Start with a simple search of one or two terms and then use locations or names to narrow your searches.
Search terms You can search using any term(s) but we recommended:
  • Report title
  • Date
  • Location
  • Type
  • Area of interest
Advanced search You can use boolean search by typing 'AND', 'NOT', 'OR' into your search. So for example you could search 'London AND South NOT Sussex NOT Surrey'
